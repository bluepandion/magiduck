1. How to play

	Arrow keys	=	Walk / Look down
	Z			=	Jump / Hold to fly
	X			=	Shoot
	
	ESC			=	Back in menu / Pause menu
	ENTER		=	Select in menu
	
	F1			=	Quick Help
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
	
2. Minimum system requirements

	IBM Compatible PC with:
	- Intel 8088, 8086 or higher.
	- CGA compatible graphics adapter.
	- 256 KB RAM.
	- 120 KB free disk space.
	- MS-DOS 3.0 or higher.
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

3. Starting the game
	
	Just type DUCK to start the game.
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

4. Command line options
	
	It's possible to override the automatic video adapter detection with
	command line parameters. Here are the options for each possible adapter:
	
	Cga		DUCK CGA
	Ega		DUCK EGA
	Vga		DUCK VGA
	Tandy	DUCK TANDY
	PCJr	DUCK PCJR
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
	
5. DosBox
	
	MagiDuck should run fine in DosBox 0.74 with just about any settings,
	except for monochrome video adapters.
	
	Recommended cycle count: 1000
